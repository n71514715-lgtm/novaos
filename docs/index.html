Built different.

Every feature exists for one reason — your privacy.

🔐
NovaCrypt
ChaCha20-Poly1305 + AES-256-XTS + Argon2id. Stronger than Telegram. Fully open source. Every file encrypted with battle-tested algorithms.
🧠
NovaBrain AI
Phi-3 Mini running locally via llama.cpp. Ask it anything. It never phones home. Your thoughts stay on your device. Always.
👁️
nova-guard
Real-time encryption watchdog. Monitors your directories and auto-encrypts new files the moment they're created. No other OS has this.
🛡️
nova-vpn
WireGuard VPN built in. One command generates your keypair. One command starts the tunnel. ChaCha20 encrypted. Zero logs.
📱
Android Support
Runs rootless on any Android 7+ phone via Termux. No root required. One command install. Your phone becomes a privacy device.
🪟
Windows App Support
Wine 10.0 built in. Run Windows applications on NovaOS. Full compatibility layer. Your tools, your OS, your rules.

Install NovaOS

One command. Any Android phone. No root required.

curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/n71514715-lgtm/novaos/main/termux/nova-quick-install.sh | bash

Requires Termux from F-Droid · Android 7.0+ · No root · No accounts



Or clone and build from source:

git clone https://github.com/n71514715-lgtm/novaos

Why NovaOS exists.

Because your operating system shouldn't spy on you. Every major OS today phones home. Windows sends telemetry. Android reports to Google. macOS checks your apps with Apple. Even your privacy tools run on infrastructure owned by the same companies watching you.

Because encryption should be the default, not the exception. NovaOS encrypts everything — your files, your communications, your AI queries — using open, audited algorithms. Not proprietary protocols. Not "trust us" encryption. Real cryptography.

Because AI shouldn't require surrendering your data. NovaBrain runs entirely on your device. Your questions, your thoughts, your conversations — none of it leaves. The model weights sit on your SSD. The inference happens on your CPU. The cloud never sees a byte.

Because the people who need privacy the most can least afford it. NovaOS runs on a $30 Android phone. It costs nothing. It requires no technical knowledge to install. It belongs to everyone.
